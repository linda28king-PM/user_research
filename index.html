For in-person moderated interviews. Total ~60 min, with the last 12 min for hands-on feature testing (test device provided).

1

暖场与背景了解Warm-up & Background~5 min

建立信任，了解购物习惯与平台使用基础Build rapport, understand shopping habits and platform baseline

  • Q1 你平时最常在哪些平台网购？Shopee 大概占你网购的多少比例？ Which platforms do you usually shop on? Roughly what share of your online shopping happens on Shopee? 最常买哪些品类？主要用手机还是电脑？ Which categories do you buy most often? Mobile or desktop?
  • Q2 你在 Shopee 上一般怎么开始找东西？能描述一下最近一次购物的经历吗？ How do you typically start looking for something on Shopee? Can you walk me through your most recent purchase? 从哪里进入？搜索还是刷首页？最后怎么决定买的？ Search bar or homepage feed? How did you make your final decision?
  • Q3 你平均一周会打开 Shopee 几次？有没有"逛"的习惯，还是一般有需求才来？ How many times a week do you open Shopee? Do you browse for fun, or mostly when you have a specific need?

Shopee 搜索使用习惯与痛点Shopee Search Habits & Pain Points~12 min

搜索词习惯、长 Query、结果相关性、筛选器、决策因素Query habits, long queries, result relevance, filters, decision factors

  • Q4 你在搜索框里一般会输入什么类型的词？能举几个最近的例子吗？ What kind of things do you typically type in the search bar? Can you give a few recent examples? 关键词有多具体？品牌名、功能描述，还是描述想解决的问题？ How specific are your queries? Brand names, feature descriptions, or problem-based queries?
  • Q5 长 Query在 Shopee 上，大家一般只搜很短的词（比如"Sepatu"）。在什么具体场景下，你会愿意输入一整句话来搜索？ Long QueryPeople usually type very short keywords on Shopee (e.g. "Sepatu"). In what specific situations would you type a full sentence to search? 如果不想打字，这时候会不会考虑用语音搜索来说出这个长需求？ If you didn't want to type it out, would you consider using voice search instead?
  • Q6 搜完结果出来后，你通常先看什么？什么让你决定点进一个商品？ After results load, what do you look at first? What makes you decide to click on an item? 图片、价格、评分、评论数、标签……优先级是什么？ Images, price, rating, review count, labels — what's your priority order?
  • Q7 当搜索结果和你想找的东西不太相关时，你一般怎么处理？ When search results aren't very relevant to what you're looking for, what do you usually do? 换词搜索？用筛选器？放弃？去别的平台？能举个例子吗？ Change keywords? Use filters? Give up? Go to another platform? Can you give an example?
  • Q8 你会用筛选功能吗（价格区间、评分、品牌等）？什么情况下会用？ Do you use filter functions (price range, rating, brand)? When do you use them, and when don't you bother? 有没有觉得现有的筛选选项不够用，或者很难找到你想要的条件？ Have you felt the current filter options are insufficient or hard to find?
  • Q9 购买商品时，你最看重哪些因素？不同品类有没有差别？ What are the main factors you consider when making a purchase on Shopee? Does this differ by category? 价格、物流时效、退换货政策、品牌、评论……不同品类的权重一样吗？ Price, delivery time, return policy, brand, reviews — how does the weighting change across categories?
  • Q10 图文混合当你看到一张图、想找"同款但换个花纹"或"匹配的配件"，你现在怎么做？会想到在 Shopee 里用图 + 文字一起搜吗？ MultimodalWhen you see an image and want "the same style in a different pattern" or "accessories to match," how do you do that today? Would you think to combine image + text in Shopee search? 有没有遇到"图不对色、图不对码"，用图搜完全搜不到你想要的那种情况？ Have you ever felt image search returns visually off results — wrong color, wrong style — and you couldn't express what you actually wanted?

  • Q12 视频/直播搜索结果页里视频卡和直播卡目前有哪些不足？你希望在展示方式、内容呈现或互动功能上做哪些改进？ Video/LiveWhat are the current limitations of video and live cards in search results? 注意倾听：用户在哪个环节"离开"了 Shopee？他们用外部工具完成的是什么任务（品类知识、比较型号、寻求背书、找灵感）？这直接指向 AI Search 可以补足的场景。 Listen for: At which point does the user "leave" Shopee? What tasks do they outsource — category education, model comparison, social validation, inspiration? These point to gaps AI Search can fill.

AI 辅助搜索功能的态度与需求Attitudes Toward AI-Assisted Search Features~10 min

推荐理由卡片、场景化搜索、AI 总结信任度、对话式购物Recommendation reasons, lifestyle search mindset, AI summary trust, conversational shopping

  • Q21 推荐理由如果商品卡片有一句"为什么推荐这款"（比如"高性价比 · 2000+ 好评"），你会不会更容易点进去？什么样的理由有用，什么样的像广告噱头？ Rec ReasonIf item cards showed a short reason explaining why they're recommended (e.g. "Great value · 2000+ reviews"), would that make you more likely to click? What kinds of reasons feel useful vs. like marketing fluff?
  • Q22 场景化搜索你有没有在 Shopee 搜索过"生活问题"而不是"商品名"？比如"漏水屋顶怎么修"或"干皮护肤食谱"——结果怎么样？有没有找到有用的商品，还是放弃去 Google 了？ Lifestyle SearchHave you ever searched for a "lifestyle problem" on Shopee instead of a product name? (e.g. "how to fix a leaking roof" or "skincare recipe for dull skin") — Did you get useful product results, or did you give up and go to Google? 把"场景化搜索流量"留在 Shopee 内部的潜力有多大？ How much potential is there to retain "lifestyle search" traffic inside Shopee rather than losing it to Google?
  • Q23 AI 信任如果 AI 总结没有标注信息来源（比如没有说具体哪家店的评价），你会觉得它不可信吗？什么样的标注方式能让你更放心？ AI TrustIf the AI summary doesn't show which specific reviews or product descriptions it extracted info from, would you consider it less trustworthy? What kind of source citation would make you more comfortable? 如果在 AI 总结里加类似 [1][2] 的小角标、点击能直接跳转到具体买家好评，能完全消除你的顾虑吗？ If we added footnote markers like [1][2] that link directly to specific buyer reviews, would that fully resolve your concerns?
  • Q24 横向信任和你平时用的 ChatGPT、TikTok AI、Google Gemini 相比，你觉得 Shopee 的 AI 总结会更可信、差不多，还是不太可信？为什么？ Trust BenchmarkCompared to general AI tools you use (e.g. ChatGPT, TikTok AI, Google Gemini), would you consider Shopee's AI summary more trustworthy, roughly the same, or less trustworthy? Why? 电商平台自己的 AI，在商品专业度上是不是比 ChatGPT 更有天然优势？ Does an e-commerce platform's own AI have a natural advantage in product knowledge vs general-purpose ChatGPT?
  • Q25 对话式购物如果在 Shopee 搜索时可以来回说清楚你的需求（比如"帮我找 150k 以内、免运费的干皮补水面霜"），你觉得什么场景下最有用？你会用吗？ ConversationalIf Shopee search let you express your needs in a back-and-forth (e.g. "Find a moisturiser for dry skin under 150k IDR with free shipping"), in what scenarios would that be most useful? Would you actually use it? 你平时更习惯点右上角的筛选器，还是会注意到并使用 AI 对话框？ Do you habitually tap the filter icon, or would you notice and use an AI chat box instead?

A · 图搜 & 图文搜A · Image Search & Multimodal Search
引导任务Guided task
  • 请用相机图标搜索一件你最近看到的、想买但不知道叫什么名字的东西。Use the camera icon to search for something you recently saw and wanted to buy but didn't know the name of.
  • 试试在图片上加一段文字描述，比如"这个款式但要蓝色"。Try adding a text description to the image, e.g. "this style but in blue."
  • Q26 你平时多久会用一次图搜？一般在什么场景下想到用它？Shopee 的图搜体验和 TikTok、Tokopedia 等平台相比怎么样？ How often do you use image search? In what situations do you think of using it? How does Shopee's image search compare to TikTok, Tokopedia, or other platforms?
  • Q27 用图搜时，你更想找到"完全一样的款"、"风格相似的商品"，还是"和这件搭配的单品"？这三类需求哪个最常见？ When using image search, do you prefer finding the exact same product, visually similar products, or complementary items? Which of these three needs comes up most often? 不同品类是否对应不同需求类型？时尚 vs 家居 vs 电子产品？ Does the need type vary by category — fashion vs home goods vs electronics?
  • Q28 你有没有在 Shopee 用过图文混搜（图片 + 文字一起搜）？什么场景下会用？目前体验满意吗？ Have you ever used multimodal search on Shopee (image + text together)? In what situations would you use it? Are you satisfied with the current experience? 有没有遇到"图搜出来颜色/款式不对，但又不知道怎么用文字补充"的情况？ Have you ever felt stuck — image search returned wrong results but you couldn't figure out how to add text to fix it?
B · 语音搜索B · Voice Search
引导任务Guided task
  • 请点击麦克风图标，用平时说话的方式搜索一件东西。Tap the microphone icon and search for something by speaking naturally.
  • 试试说一句比较长的需求，比如"推荐一款适合干皮、150k 以内的保湿乳"。Try saying a longer request: e.g. "recommend a moisturiser for dry skin under 150k."
  • Q29 你有没有在 Shopee 用过语音搜索？平时什么情况下你会选择说话而不是打字？这次体验下来，你会推荐给朋友吗？为什么？ Have you used voice search on Shopee before? When would you choose speaking over typing? After trying it today, would you recommend it to a friend? Why or why not? 识别准确吗？搜出来的结果符合你的预期吗？ Was the recognition accurate? Did results match what you meant?
C · AI Search Summary
引导任务Guided task
  • 请搜索一个你最近想买、但还没决定买哪款的东西，看看页面顶部有什么。Search for something you've been considering but haven't decided on — notice what appears at the top of results.
  • Q30 （AI Summary 出现后）你注意到这段总结了吗？第一反应是什么？你会先读它，还是直接往下滑看商品？ (After AI Summary appears) Did you notice this summary? What was your first reaction? Did you read it first, or scroll past to the product list?
  • Q31 AI 总结推荐了 3 款商品，但不一定完全符合你的预算。你会用传统的筛选器手动过滤，还是直接告诉 AI"给我看 150k 以内的"？ The AI summary recommended 3 products but they may not match your budget. Would you use traditional filters manually, or simply tell the AI "show me cheaper ones under 150k IDR"? 用户是习惯去点右上角的筛选器，还是会注意到 AI 对话框进行多轮交互？ Does the user instinctively tap the top-right filter icon, or notice and engage with the AI chat box for multi-turn refinement?
  • Q32 如果 AI 总结把某款推荐为"最高性价比"，但你点进去发现它不支持 Gratis Ongkir（免运费），这会怎么影响你对 AI 的信任？ If the AI summary recommends a product as "Best Value," but you find it doesn't support Gratis Ongkir (Free Shipping), how would that affect your trust in the AI? 印尼买家对运费极其敏感——AI 推荐和本地"免邮利益"打架时，用户的流失率会有多高？ Indonesian buyers are highly sensitive to shipping costs — how much does a mismatch between AI recommendations and "free shipping" affect drop-off?
  • Q33 如果 AI 总结完美地回答了你的问题、并推荐了 3 款合适的商品，你还会继续往下滑看传统的商品列表吗？为什么？ If the AI summary perfectly answers your query and recommends 3 right products, would you still scroll down to browse the traditional product listings below? Why or why not? 评估 AI Search 对传统 SRP 瀑布流广告位曝光以及用户滑动行为的覆盖程度。 This assesses how much AI Search cannibalizes traditional SRP ad impressions and changes organic scrolling behavior.
  • Q34 你觉得这段 AI 总结是谁生成的？知道是 AI 写的，会影响你对它的信任吗？ Who do you think generated this AI summary? Does knowing it's AI-written change how much you trust it?
  • Q35 （收尾）如果以后每次搜索都有 AI 帮你总结、图文混搜也更精准，你觉得这会改变你用 Shopee 搜索和购物的方式吗？ (Closing) If every search came with AI summaries and multimodal search worked more precisely, do you think that would change how you search and shop on Shopee?
重点观察：① 图搜偏好——同款 vs 相似款 vs 搭配款；② 图文搜的自然使用率；③ AI Summary 是否改变点击行为；④ 用户是否主动尝试对话式筛选还是依赖传统筛选器；⑤ Ongkir 信息冲突对信任的实际冲击。 Key observations: ① Image search preference — exact match vs similar vs complementary; ② Natural adoption of multimodal search; ③ Whether AI Summary changes click behavior; ④ Conversational filtering vs traditional filters; ⑤ Real impact of Ongkir mismatch on trust.
